Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

A prosperous industrial city in Pennsylvania. Pittsburgh is associated with some of the wealthiest and most powerful families in America, such as the Carnegies and Mellons (steel and power generation), the Fricks (stockholdings) and Heinzs (food-canning). Coincidentally, today the seat of the internationally renowned Borges Center

Source: Fishburn, Evelyn, and Psiche Hughes. A Dictionary of Borges.
