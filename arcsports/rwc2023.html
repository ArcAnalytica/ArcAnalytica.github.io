Three measures to predict who will win the Rugby World Cup 2023

In this article we will analyze three factors regarding the top ten ranked squads for the Rugby World Cup 2023 in France.

Age

The first measure we will look at is age. By taking an average age of all 33 members in the squad, which nation has a "young squad?" Which has an "old squad?"

Experience

Experience, which will be measured here by the number of international caps is also an important factor.

Form

Lastly, form, or the win-loss-draw ratio coming into the tournament is the last factor to be considered here.
